Strategic Overview

Market Opportunity

The total addressable market for EU-to-US expansion services stands at $2.5-4B. Annually, between 7,500 and 10,500 European companies initiate US expansion, creating a critical demand for specialized tax and advisory services.

Strategic Approach

We recommend a Partner-Led Sales Model leveraging trusted advisors (VCs, Law Firms) to minimize CAC and build trust rapidly. This approach capitalizes on Orbiss's unique value proposition: speed (48hr SLA) and specialized expertise.
